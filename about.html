ABOUT ITALY

la storia · l'anima · la bellezza

🇮🇹 The Heart of the Mediterranean

Italy is a country in southern Europe, known for its rich history, cultural and historical landscapes. Its capital city, Rome, was once the center of the powerful Roman Empire. Italy is famous for its art and architecture, with great artists like Leonardo da Vinci and Michelangelo.

From the snow-capped Alps in the north to the sun-baked shores of Sicily, Italy's diverse geography has shaped its unique regional identities. The country is also well known for its delicious food, such as pizza and pasta, as well as traditional Italian desserts like tiramisu and panna cotta.

📜 A Journey Through Time

⚔️ Roman Empire

From 753 BC, Rome grew into one of history's greatest empires, leaving roads, law, and architecture.

🎨 The Renaissance

Florence and Venice spark a rebirth of art, science, and humanism (14th-17th century).

🏛️ Unification

Italy became a unified nation-state in 1861, with Rome as its capital.

🌍 Modern Italy

A founding member of the EU, known for fashion, design, and la dolce vita.
